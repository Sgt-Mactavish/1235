INDIA POST 2022 RECRUITMENT | GDS POST | Job Details | POST OFFICE | Age Limit | Educational Qualification | Apply Link | Freshers Job


1. Job Profile : India Post is under the Department of Post, Ministry of Communications of the Government of India releases PAN india notifications for following posts :-
a) BRANCH POSTMASTER (BPM).
b) ASSISTANT BRANCH POSTMASTER (ABPM).
c) DAK SEVAK



2. No. of posts :



Total : 38926 Nos. as BPM/ABPM/DAK SEVAK.

3. Engagement Schedule : Last date of application 05/06/2022.

4. Salary (per month) :
a) For BPM : Rs.12,000/-
b) ABPM/Dak Sevak : Rs.10,000/-

5. AGE LIMIT

  1. Minimum age: 18 years on or before 05th June 2022.
  2. Maximum age:40 years on or before 05th June 2022.
  3. Relaxations in Maximum age limit for different categories are: -
    • Schedule Cast/Scheduled Tribe (SC/ST)5 Years
    • Other Backward Classes (OBC) 3 Years.
    • Economically Weaker Sections (EWS) No relaxation.
    • Persons with Disabilities (PwD) 10 Years.
    • Persons with Disabilities (PwD) + OBC 13 Years
    • Persons with Disabilities (PwD) + SC/ST 15 Years.


6. Educational Qualification : Should have passed Secondary Examination (10th Std.) with certificate and Mathematics and English should have compulsory or elective subjects.

7. Other Qualifications : Compulsory knowledge of Local Language, the candidate should have studied the local language at least up to 10th standard and Knowledge of Cycling, scooter or motor cycle.


8. Application Fee : : Candidates have to pay a fee of Rs.100/- (Rupees one hundred only) Note:- Payment of fee is exempted for all female candidates, SC/ST candidates, PwD candidates and Transwomen candidates.
