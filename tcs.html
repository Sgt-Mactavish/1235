Educational Qualification:

TCS Off Campus Hiring for Year of pass out 2020, 2021 and 2022:
  1. Percentage: Students must have minimum aggregate marks (Every subjects and in all semesters) of 60% or 6 CGPA in each of Class Xth, Class XIIth, Diploma (if applicable), Graduation and Post-Graduation examinations.

  2. Highest Qualification: Students must complete their education within the course duration (i.e. No extended education).
  3. Backlogs: Students must not have any pending backlogs and arrears.
  4. Gap in Education: It is mandatory to declare gaps in education, if there is any. Overall academic gap should not exceed 2 years until highest qualification. Required document proof will be checked for academics gaps in education.
  5. Course Types: Only full-time courses will be considered (Part-time courses will not be considered)Students who have completed their Secondary and if any Senior Secondary course from National Institute of Open Schooling are also eligible to apply for the drive if the other courses are full-time.
  6. Work Experience: Freshers and who have work experience of up to 2 years are eligible to apply for the drive.
  7. Age: A student should be of 18 - 28 years of age to participate in the TCS Off Campus Hiring process.
  8. Degree : Students from B.E or B.Tech ,M.E or M.Tech/MCA/M.Sc from any branch offered by a recognized university/college are eligible to apply.
